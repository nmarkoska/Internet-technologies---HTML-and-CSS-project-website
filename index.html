Let's start with some important things about the Corona Virus

COVID-19, or more popularly THE CORONA VIRUS, is the disease caused by the new coronavirus that emerged in China in December 2019. COVID-19 symptoms include cough, fever, shortness of breath, muscle aches, sore throat, unexplained loss of taste or smell, diarrhea and headache. COVID-19 can be severe, and some cases have caused death The new coronavirus can be spread from person to person.

As my recommendation, and also from the World Health Organization, I ask you all to respect the measures to stay safe.

  1. Stay home as much as possible and reduce visitors.
  2. Wash your hands with soap and water frequently and thoroughly for at least 20 seconds.
  3. Avoid touching your eyes, nose or mouth, especially with unwashed hands.
  4. Always wear a protective mask and gloves, especially indoors where other people are present, such as shops, markets and malls.
